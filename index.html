An Introduction to Typedash

Here’s a quick look into what Typedash can do.

News

Custom news feed featuring our favorite tech blogs and newspapers. Currently featuring The Verge, TechCrunch, Wired and more.

Custom Look

Choose from Typedash’s list of favorite fonts to customize your dashboard. Choose background colors too. Themes coming soon!

More

Quickly connect to your favorite social networks, check the weather to make sure you cargo shorts are weather appropriate.

Your dashboard, your look

One of the founding reasons we started Typedash was to create a dashboard that looked the way we wanted it to. We plan to roll out new colors and fonts in the coming months as well as custom themes by our favorite designers and illustrators.
